⚔️ [ RPG Dungeon Guide ] PLAY!

The #RPG channel is a full MUD-style text adventure run by DungeonMaster. It's a cyberpunk dungeon set inside the Raspberry Pi's Linux filesystem. /dev/null is the void, /proc is the hall of processes, /tmp is the fleamarket, /boot is the ancient temple.

Getting Started

1. Join #RPG on the IRC server
2. Type /new [class] to create a character
3. Classes: hacker (high ATK), sysadmin (balanced), script (fast/chaotic), daemon (tanky)
4. Type /look to see your surroundings
5. Type /go north to explore
6. Type /fight to battle monsters
7. Or just type anything — the DM interprets it as a free-form action!

Command Reference

/helpShow help overview in IRC
/commandsList all available commands
/new [class]Create character (hacker/sysadmin/script/daemon)
/lookExamine your surroundings (alias: /l)
/go <dir>Move: north/south/east/west or location name
/mapShow the ASCII dungeon map
/fightBattle a random monster (alias: /f)
/healUse a healing potion
/use <item>Use or equip an item from inventory
/take <item>Pick up an item from the ground
/drop <item>Drop an item from inventory
/inventoryCheck your items (alias: /inv, /i)
/statsView your character sheet (alias: /st)
/say <msg>Speak in-character
/yell <msg>Shout to all adventurers
/whoList active adventurers
/historyYour recent adventure log
/scenario <desc>Seed a custom scene (affects entire dungeon)
/dm <desc>Trigger a DM battle encounter
/resetReset the entire world (use with caution!)

Monsters & Loot

Monsters are corrupt processes & rogue daemons: Zombie Processes, Fork Bombs, Memory Leaks, Segfault Specters, Buffer Overflows, the OOM Killer, Kernel Panic, and more. Defeat them for XP, gold, and rare drops. Loot includes CPU crystals, mutex locks, daemon souls, kernel modules, and the legendary sudo amulet.

Start at The Boot Sector. Low danger areas: Home District, Cache Bazaar, Config Library. High danger: The Void (/dev/null), OOM Arena, Swap Caverns, Kernel Throne. The DM adjusts encounters to your level. Free-form actions earn bonus XP!

DM Modes

/scenario — Set a scene that affects the entire dungeon. Example: /scenario A power surge ripples through the Pi. All lights flicker. A new boss emerges from /dev/sda. Everyone in the dungeon feels the scenario's effects.

/dm — Trigger a custom battle or event. Example: /dm An army of fork bombs floods the corridor The DM narrates the battle with damage and outcomes based on your stats.
